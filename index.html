What is a Core Banking System?

A Core Banking System (CBS) is the backbone of modern financial institutions, enabling SACCOs to manage customer accounts, transactions, loans, deposits, and other SACCO operations in real time. With a CBS, banking services become more efficient, secure, and accessible, allowing customers to perform transactions from anywhere whether through mobile banking or online platforms.

Key Features & Modules

Dashboard Module

A centralized interface that provides a real-time overview of the SACCO’s operations, including total deposits, loans, transactions, and customer activity. It helps management make data-driven decisions with insights and analytics.

Clients Module

Manages customer profiles, including personal details, account types, transaction history, KYC (Know Your Customer) compliance, and banking preferences. This module ensures seamless client onboarding and efficient customer service.

Loans Module

Handles loan application, approval, disbursement, and repayment tracking. It supports different loan types, interest calculations, and automated reminders for due payments.

Savings Module

Enables customers to open and manage savings accounts, track deposits and withdrawals, and earn interest. The system also supports automated savings plans and fixed deposit schemes.

Shares Module

Designed for SACCOs and credit unions, this module manages member shares, dividends, and equity contributions. It ensures transparency in shareholding and financial growth tracking.

School Pay Module

A dedicated module for processing school fees payments, allowing parents and students to pay fees directly through banks, mobile money, or agents. It generates receipts and automates reconciliation for schools.

Accounting Module

Provides a full-fledged financial management system, handling general ledger, accounts payable & receivable, balance sheets, and profit & loss statements. This ensures regulatory compliance and accurate financial reporting.

SMS Banking

Enables customers to receive instant SMS notifications for transactions, account updates, loan reminders, and promotional messages. It enhances customer engagement and reduces fraud risks.

Mobile Banking

Allows customers to perform banking transactions via a mobile app or USSD, including fund transfers, bill payments, loan requests, and account inquiries, ensuring banking access anytime, anywhere.

Agent Banking

Extends banking services to remote areas through authorized agents. Agents can perform cash deposits, withdrawals, loan repayments, and account inquiries on behalf of customers.

Mobile Money

Enables customers to transfer funds directly to mobile phones or virtual accounts, reducing the need for separate bank accounts. It also supports mobile money withdrawals and transfers.

Data Importer

Facilitates seamless migration and integration of data from other banking systems or third-party platforms, ensuring a smooth transition for institutions adopting the Core CBS software.

Reporting Module

Generates detailed reports on transactions, customer activity, loan performance, savings growth, and regulatory compliance. Customizable report templates help management monitor financial health and make strategic decisions.
